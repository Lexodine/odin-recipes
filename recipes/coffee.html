Description

A perfect cappuccino has a velvety texture and just the right ratio of espresso, milk, and, most importantly, foam..

Ingridients

  • fresh coffee beans
  • hot water
  • milk
  • vanilla

steps

  1. Heat water to boiling point
  2. grind beans on low setting
  3. pour water over ground coffee
  4. add milk and vanilla
